100 Value Point At A Glance

1. Register


Register via online form or emailing of form before dateline.

Participating fee: 100 Euro per wine

Registration is successful only after you receive the confirmation email and delivery label.

2. Samples Reception


Samples must be sent to designated address before dateline.

Requirement:

Only applicable to bottled wines, 2 bottles per registered wine with technical sheets.

3. Tasting of wines

The 3 tasting panel will taste the wines together. Different vintage will be tasted on different days.

4. Organiser receives reports

The 3 tasting panel will submit their completed reports to the organiser within 1 week from the tasting period.

5. Results

Announcement of results via online and Email. Results will be uploaded by organiser within 1 week from reception of reports.
